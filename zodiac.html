Zodiac Signs and Elements

The zodiac contains twelve signs divided among four elements: fire, earth, air, and water.

The Four Zodiac Elements

Zodiac signs grouped by element and common characteristics
Element Zodiac Signs Common Characteristics
Fire Aries, Leo, Sagittarius Energetic, expressive, passionate
Earth Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn Practical, steady, dependable
Air Gemini, Libra, Aquarius Social, thoughtful, communicative
Water Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces Emotional, intuitive, compassionate

Fire and Earth

Fire signs are often associated with action and enthusiasm. Earth signs are associated with stability and practical thinking.

Air and Water

Air signs are associated with ideas and communication. Water signs are associated with emotion and intuition.
