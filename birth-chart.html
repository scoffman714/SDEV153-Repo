Understanding Your Birth Chart

A birth chart shows the positions of the Sun, Moon, planets, zodiac signs, and houses at a particular moment.

The Main Parts of a Birth Chart

  • Planets: Represent different drives and needs.
  • Signs: Describe how planetary energy is expressed.
  • Houses: Represent different areas of life.
  • Aspects: Show relationships between planets.

The Sun

The Sun is associated with identity, purpose, and self-expression.

The Moon

The Moon is associated with emotions, instincts, and inner needs.

The Rising Sign

The rising sign is associated with first impressions and how a person approaches new situations.
