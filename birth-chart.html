Understanding Your Birth Chart

A birth chart shows the positions of the Sun, Moon, planets, zodiac signs, and houses at a particular moment.

The Main Parts of a Birth Chart

  • Planets: Represent different drives and needs.
  • Signs: Describe how planetary energy is expressed.
  • Houses: Represent different areas of life.
  • Aspects: Show relationships between planets.

The Sun

The Sun is associated with identity, purpose, and self-expression.

The Moon

The Moon is associated with emotions, instincts, and inner needs.

The Rising Sign

The rising sign is associated with first impressions and how a person approaches new situations.

Understanding Planetary Aspects

Planetary aspects describe the angles that planets form with one another in a birth chart. These connections show how the energies of the planets interact, either harmoniously or with some tension.

Conjunction

A conjunction occurs when two planets are positioned very close together. Their energies combine and become more powerful. Depending on the planets involved, this combination can feel supportive, intense, or challenging.

Opposition

An opposition occurs when two planets are located directly across from one another in the birth chart. It can create tension between two different needs or areas of life, encouraging balance and compromise.

Trine

A trine is a harmonious aspect that allows the energies of two planets to work together naturally. It often represents talents, strengths, or areas of life that feel comfortable and easy.

Square

A square is a challenging aspect that may create conflict, frustration, or obstacles. Although squares can feel difficult, they can also encourage personal growth, action, and change.

Sextile

A sextile is a supportive aspect that represents opportunities, cooperation, and potential. Its positive energy may require some effort or initiative before its benefits are fully experienced.
